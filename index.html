Security & Privacy

Your data stays in your system.

All work is performed inside your existing CRM platform using access credentials you provide and control. No client data is exported, downloaded, copied, or stored outside your system. Access can be revoked by you at any time.

Compliance:

  • Spam Act 2003 (Cth)
  • Australian Privacy Principles (Privacy Act 1988)
  • Do Not Call Register Act 2006

We do not download or store loan documents, financial statements, or sensitive client records. We work with contact records, loan status fields, and settlement dates only.
